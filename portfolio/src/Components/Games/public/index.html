Bad Apple HP

You're a developer looking for a job. You have $10k and must make it through the daunting three phase application and interview process to get that job. While you're at it you’ll lose $200 every 10 seconds, so it's important that you work fast or you might end up with no job and no money!

In the recruiter phase you’ll meet some good recruiters (wearing green) and some bad apple recruiters (wearing yellow). Advancing to the next phase requires you to pass five resumes to good recruiters and toss five apples at bad apple recruiters. Each time you make a mistake you’ll lose one hit point from the category of recruiter you made the mistake with.

The phone interview is a multiple choice test. Passing this test means you’ll advance to the tech interview. A failure sends you back to the recruiter phase and resets your hit points.

The tech interview involves solving the famous FizzBuzz problem using JavaScript. Passing this phase results in a job offer. A failure sends you back to the recruiter phase and resets your hit points.

Running out of money means you become homeless. Good luck out there!
